Saqa Pandega Adha Dananjaya, also known as Danan, is a student and emerging visual artist from Sidoarjo, Indonesia. His works focus on human behavior, especially on how people respond to problems in unusual or irrational ways. He is interested in how these patterns of thought continue to exist in society, often shaped by generational shifts and political influence. Social and political themes often appear in his works, showing how power can affect the way people think and act. Through drawing, installation, music, and digital media, He creates art as a way to raise awareness and open space for honest conversation about the issues around us.
